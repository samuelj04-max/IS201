About Tiger Woods

Tiger Woods is one of the most successful and influential golfers in history, known for revolutionizing the sport with his dominance and athleticism. He turned professional in 1996 and quickly rose to fame, winning the Masters Tournament in 1997 by a record-breaking margin at just 21 years old. Over his career, he has won 15 major championships and has spent over 600 weeks as the world’s number one ranked golfer. Despite facing serious injuries and personal challenges, Woods made a historic comeback by winning the 2019 Masters, one of the most celebrated moments in sports history.
